About Us

Japanese Restaurant · Toronto Theatre District

Located in the heart of Toronto's theatre district, Funé has been operating since the nineties — a quiet institution built on craft and consistency.

We are committed to creating exquisite Japanese delicacies. Our assortment of sushi is meticulously executed, supremely fresh, and punctuated with welcome surprises that keep guests returning season after season.

Whether you prefer the classical elegance of traditional Japanese cuisine or the bold expression of neo-modern dishes, every plate is prepared with our heart and skills — to satisfy your love of quality Japanese food.

Our revolving sushi bar is a beloved centrepiece: a living procession of handcrafted pieces that invites discovery, conversation, and the pleasure of eating at your own pace.
